Welcome to Ibadan Tech Expo 2024 – a dynamic and vibrant gathering that
celebrates the world of technology and innovation in Ibadan, Nigeria’s

enthusiasts, professionals, and stakeholders. Whether you’re a seasoned
industry veteran or a newcomer looking to explore the exciting possibilities
of the tech scene, this event is tailor-made for you.
